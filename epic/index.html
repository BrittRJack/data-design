User Story

As a user, Nick is an up and coming fashion designer who's is contracted by a major clothing company.
He likes to visit other clothing lines' websites to keep current in the industry.

Use Case:

Title: Nick's user page on H&M website

Description: Nick wants to gain insight by viewing changing clothing trends via H&M

Name of user and their role: Nick; fashion guru who wants to showcase his designs on the internet

Interaction Flow:

Nick signs into H&M website via user profile sign in button.
Nick clicks 'men's latest trends'

Nick types 'Men's Jackets' in search bar. He is brought to the page with a photo slide presentation.
He clicks on the photos to load them.

Nick can view each photo and see how many views each photo has received.
He can also see which designer uploaded them.

Nick can now add the clothing itmes he likes to his own user page and save them for later viewing.

Nick can also share his favorite items with the'share' link to his social media sites.
