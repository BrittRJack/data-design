Conceptual Model

Entities and Attributes

Designers:

  • designerId (Primary key)
  • designerType
  • designerBrand
  • designerProductId

Customers:

  • userId (Primary Key)
  • userName
  • userActivation
  • userEmail

Products

  • productId (Primary Key)
  • productType
  • productLists
  • productItems

Categories:

  • categoryId (Foreign Key)
  • categoryLists
  • categoryItems
  • categoryProductId (Foreign Key)

Relationships

1 to 1 relationship: One designer can only have one page.

Many to many: Many customers can buy from many designers
